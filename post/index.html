Project 2: Etherium Price Prediction

Cryptocurrency is the future of online currency and traditional trading. Due to the recent rise of technology and market changes, everyone wants to buy cryptocurrency. I have build a Deep Neural Network model to predict Ethereum closing price for future dates based on historical data.I got the historical data of ethereum from 18 December,2017 to 16 April, 2021 from https://www.cryptodatadownload.com/data/binance/. This data is sorted by time and recorded at equal intervals (1-day). Such a sequence of data is called Time Series. When we do Time Series forecasting, the value of current time step affects the next one. There are many methods that you can use for this purpose. But I build a Deep Neural Network to predict future Ethereum price.

Project 3: NLP based womens clothing reviews

Project 4: Telco churn analysis

Simply put, customer churn rate is the inability of a company to retain its customers over a given period of time. There are a number of actions and activities certain companies take to reduce churn and increase customer retention. Focusing on customer retention is important because it not only looks at how good a company is at acquiring new customers but also how good they are at keeping those customers. While you may have the best acquisition process in the business, if your retention is terrible then it’s all worthless.
